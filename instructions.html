How to Play

You control a GREEN dot on the screen. GOLD will randomly appear somewhere on the screen every now and then. Collecting them increases your score. You NEED all of it... Be careful, though, as obstacles (indicated by RED) will appear and they'll deal damage to you when they hit you. You have 3 HP. Get as much gold as you can. It all belongs to you and nobody else...

The game gets more difficult as time passes. There's also power-ups that'll help you (most of the time). Just be careful not to accidentally get a dupe, which will cause a random hallucination.

WASD = Move, Arrow Keys = Dash
